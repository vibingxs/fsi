Faced with technologically-savvy consumers and agile new competitors, financial services organizations are increasingly looking to their internal IT functions to design and deliver innovative new services. But in fast-changing environment, how can you identify and prioritize the innovations that will meet the organization’s strategic objectives and deliver the best returns?

An Enterprise Project Portfolio Management (EPPM) solution can help your IT organization to balance technological innovation with the need to maintain and develop existing, business-critical systems. With a clearer view of all assets across the enterprise, IT functions can improve project selection, execution, and success-while optimizing resources to support continued innovation.

Oracle’s Instantis EnterpriseTrack is a leading, cloud-based PPM solution that can help you to:

  • Deliver winning products and services
  • Prioritize the most promising development projects
  • Optimize work and resource management
  • Facilitate continuous improvement
