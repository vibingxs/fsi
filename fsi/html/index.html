FSI Background

Financial services are in transition. With economic turbulence creating an uncertain business environment, financial services organizations need to attract and retain customers, tighten up efficiency, reduce operating costs, and gain greater visibility of their operations for watertight compliance.

At the same time, disruptive new entrants are capturing market share with innovative online and mobile solutions. Established financial services businesses must develop new services to maintain competitive advantage. That means having the means to identify the innovations that will deliver the best returns.

A third key challenge is the tightening of the global regulatory environment. New rules are being introduced and existing ones modified at an accelerating rate. Financial organizations must be able to keep up with the changes, ensure they are implemented, and demonstrate effective risk management.

For many leading institutions, an Enterprise Project Portfolio Management (EPPM) approach is the answer to these challenges. Explore the resources on this site to discover how EPPM is enabling financial services organizations to achieve strategic efficiency, deliver innovative new services, and keep pace with rapid and complicated regulatory change.
